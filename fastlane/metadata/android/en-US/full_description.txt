This app allows to view, edit and synchronise HTML notes between Android devices and IMAP servers.

* be used with other servers (Gmail, Yahoo, AOL, Posteo and your server)
* HTML editor with lots of features
* light and dark mode support
* color support for notes
* share your notes
* app can handle sticky notes used by Kerio Connect servers
* security can be plain text, SSL/TLS or STARTTLS
* multiple IMAP accounts can be managed
* works offline and sync is done in the background
* no permissions are needed by the app
